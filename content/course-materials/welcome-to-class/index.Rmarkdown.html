Learning Objectives

  1. Explain what the evidence shows regarding learning outcomes of active versus lecture-based learning.
  3. Carry out the protocol of at least one active learning strategy in your own classroom.

Preparation

Read this Washington Post article and post a follow-up comment on the class forum. Here are some guiding questions:

  • Do you agree with the sentiment of this piece? Have you ever been in a class where lecture was completely ineffective?
  • Can you offer a counterexample—are there situations where lecture is actually valuable and effective?
  • Have you ever been in a class that is not lecture-based? What are some strategies you might suggest for successful alternative teaching strategies?

Reflection

  1. Select an active learning protocol to implement in your next class: Peer Instruction, Think-Pair-Share, Purposeful Question, Quick Write, Other…

  2. Write a detailed description of how you will facilitate it, including

    • the concept question / prompt
    • timing of each element
    • precisely what students will be asked to do
    • how groups will be formed

  3. Reflect on how it went

    • how did it feel to facilitate?
    • in what ways was it effective/ineffective?
